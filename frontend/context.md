## Module 1: AI Scam Detection Engine

*The core of CyberGlimmora — catching scams before they catch you*

### Detection Channels
- **SMS messages** — fake bank alerts, OTP scams, delivery fraud, lottery scams
- **WhatsApp & Telegram** — user forwards suspicious messages for instant AI analysis
- **Email** — phishing patterns, fake invoice links, credential harvesting
- **Websites** — AI Browser Shield checks URLs for fake login pages, phishing domains, crypto scam sites
- **Screenshots** — user uploads a screenshot; AI analyzes it instantly
- **Social media messages** — fake job offers, romance scams, investment fraud in DMs

### India-Specific Scam Detection
- **UPI fraud:** Fake payment requests, collect money scams, QR code traps
- **Fake bank SMS:** Fraudulent HDFC/SBI/ICICI alerts asking users to call or click
- **KYC scams:** "Your account will be blocked — complete KYC immediately"
- **Aadhaar and PAN fraud:** Fake government portals collecting identity documents
- **Fake courier scams:** "Your package is held, pay customs fee"
- **Fake job offers:** HR impersonation, advance fee fraud
- **Crypto investment scams:** Fake trading platforms promising guaranteed returns

### Scam Screenshot Analyzer
- **Input:** Screenshot of any message, offer, or website
- **Output:** Risk Level (High / Medium / Low) + Reason + Recommended Action
- **Example:** "Risk: HIGH — Fake investment scheme. Promises guaranteed 40% monthly returns. This is a classic Ponzi pattern. Do not send money."

### Scam Simulation Training
Interactive quiz mode. App sends simulated scam messages that look real — user must identify scam vs safe. Trains users to recognize fraud instinctively. Highly engaging and shareable.

### Community Scam Intelligence Network
User-reported scams are anonymously verified and added to a live threat database. All network users are then protected within minutes. Network effect: more users = smarter, faster detection.

## Module 2: Digital Identity Guardian

*Your identity, monitored across the entire internet*

### Digital Identity Twin
A real-time model of the user's online footprint that continuously monitors for unauthorized use:
- Monitors all email addresses, phone numbers, and usernames
- Detects fake social media accounts using the user's name or photo
- Tracks where personal data has been shared or sold
- Identifies impersonation on LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook
- Monitors for leaked Aadhaar, PAN, or passport information

### Dark Web Monitoring
Continuously scans dark web marketplaces, paste sites, and hacker forums for:
- Leaked email and password combinations
- Exposed phone numbers and banking details
- Breached credit card data
- Stolen identity documents (Aadhaar, PAN, passport numbers)
- Compromised account credentials

### Breach Alert & Recovery
When a breach is detected, CyberGlimmora provides a **step-by-step recovery plan**: which passwords to change, which accounts to secure, which services to notify. Every alert is actionable, not just informational.

## Module 4: Personal Cyber Risk Score

*Know your digital safety level — and improve it*

### Score Calculation (0–100)
Five pillars:
1. **Password health** — strength, reuse, breach exposure
2. **Account security** — 2FA on email, banking, social accounts
3. **Device security** — OS updated, screen lock, risky apps
4. **Breach exposure** — known data breaches linked to user
5. **Behavioral patterns** — clicking unverified links, public Wi-Fi usage

### Actionable Score Improvement
The score is a roadmap. Below every score, users see:
- Specific factors dragging it down
- Exact steps to fix them
- Real-time score updates as actions are taken

**Example:**
- Score: 61/100 (Moderate Risk)
- Issue 1: Password reused on 5 websites → Change to unique passwords
- Issue 2: Email found in 3 breaches → Update credentials
- Issue 3: 2FA not enabled on banking app → Enable now
- Estimated After Fix: 84/100 (Low Risk)

## Module 7: Ride & Travel Safety Monitor

*AI-powered safety monitoring for every journey*

### How It Works
1. User starts ride, taps "Monitor My Ride"
2. App monitors journey in real time via GPS
3. AI compares actual route against expected fastest route
4. Detects unusual stops, long pauses, route deviations
5. Alerts trusted contacts if anomaly detected (with live GPS)
6. One-tap SOS available throughout

### Alert Types
| Alert | Description |
|---|---|
| **Route Deviation** | Driver takes unexpected route. Alert sent with live map. |
| **Unusual Long Stop** | Vehicle stationary in isolated location 5+ minutes. Auto-alert. |
| **Speed Anomaly** | Unusual speed or wrong direction. Contact notified. |
| **Arrival Confirmation** | User safely reached destination. Contacts notified. |
| **SOS Trigger** | GPS location, ride details, emergency message sent to all contacts. |

### Target Users
- Women traveling alone (especially at night) — primary audience
- Students in new/unfamiliar cities
- Parents tracking child's school commute
- Senior citizens at medical appointments
- Anyone wanting a digital safety guardian for travel
